# Code of Conduct

## The Short Version

Be kind, be respectful, be professional. We don't tolerate harassment or discrimination of any kind.

## The Longer Version

This project is dedicated to providing a harassment-free experience for everyone. We do not tolerate harassment of participants in any form.

**Expected:**
- Treat everyone with respect and kindness
- Welcome and support people of all backgrounds
- Be thoughtful in how you communicate
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully

**Not Allowed:**
- Harassment, intimidation, or offensive behavior
- Discriminatory jokes or language
- Personal attacks or trolling
- Sharing others' private information
- Anything you wouldn't do in a professional workplace

## Reporting and Enforcement

Report issues to the maintainers. Violations may result in warnings, temporary bans, or permanent removal from the project.

Based on the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/).
